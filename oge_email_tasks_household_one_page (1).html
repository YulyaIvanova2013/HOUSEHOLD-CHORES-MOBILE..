OGE Email Writing

Household chores and housework tasks

Write an email to your English-speaking pen-friend. Answer all 3 questions. Write 100–120 words. Remember the rules of email writing.

Household chores

From: Jane@mail.uk
To: Russian_friend@oge.ru
Subject: Household chores
… My parents work long hours and don’t have enough time for the household chores. My sister and I try to help them as much as we can, and our parents have more time to spend with us. …

What do you do to help your parents about the house? Who usually does the shopping in your family? What do you enjoy doing all together when you have free time? …
Write an email to Jane and answer her 3 questions.

Housework

From: Ben@mail.uk
To: Russian_friend@oge.ru
Subject: Housework
… I am a bit sad because I have just quarrelled with my mum. I didn’t want to wash the dishes and make my bed because there was an interesting film on TV. …

Do you think children should help their parents with the housework, why or why not? What do you usually do about the house? What housework do you dislike? …
Write an email to Ben and answer his 3 questions.
