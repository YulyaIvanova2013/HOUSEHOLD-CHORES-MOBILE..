Telephone survey tasks

You are going to take part in a telephone survey. You have to answer six questions. Give full answers to the questions. Remember that you have 40 seconds to answer each question.

School students’ daily routine

Hello! It’s the electronic assistant of Teenage Magazine online. We kindly ask you to take part in our survey. We would like to find out how teenagers feel about school students’ daily routine. Please answer six questions. The survey is anonymous – you don’t have to give your name. So, let’s get started.
  1. 1
    What time do you usually go to bed in the evening?
  2. 2
    How much time do you spend doing your homework?
  3. 3
    What sports do you do regularly?
  4. 4
    What do you do to help your parents about the house?
  5. 5
    How do you spend your weekends?
  6. 6
    What would you like to change in your daily routine?

Teenagers’ daily routines

Hello! It’s the electronic assistant of Teenage Magazine online. We kindly ask you to take part in our survey. We would like to find out how teenagers feel about teenagers’ daily routines. Please answer six questions. The survey is anonymous – you don’t have to give your name. So, let’s get started.
  1. 1
    When do you get up on weekdays?
  2. 2
    What do you prefer to eat for breakfast in the morning?
  3. 3
    How long does it take you to get to school?
  4. 4
    What is your favourite part of the day: morning, afternoon or evening? Why do you like it?
  5. 5
    What do you do to help your parents about the house?
  6. 6
    What would you recommend for teenagers who are often late for school?
